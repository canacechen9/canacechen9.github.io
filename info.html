Canace (Yiran, 逸然) Chen is a creative technologist, artist, and interaction/UI/UX designer based in London, UK.

She explores the intersection of art and technology, with a focus on creative technology and human–computer interaction. Her practice spans VR, AR, interactive installations, generative AI, and machine learning, where she enjoys experimenting with new ways to engage audiences.
