Regional Routes (GPJ)

Apr 22 2024 – Museum of Future, Dubai



This project is another interactive installation for Starbucks’ MENA coffee launch at the Museum of Future, Dubai. It is an interactive animation projected onto sand – inspired and by a traditional Middle Eastern method of making coffee by heating it in sand.

Guests will learn and discover more about the coffee blend using a digital immersive table: tracing the origins of coffee, it’s history in the MENA region, discover traditional serving and brewing methods and flavours.

Content and infographics plays out around the product & props. Audio or animation could be triggered by moving props onto marked areas.
