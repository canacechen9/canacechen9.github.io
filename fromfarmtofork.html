From Farm to Fork (GPJ)

Feb 7–9 2024 – Berlin

Sep 4-6 2024 – Hong Kong



This project was build for DP World at Fruit Logistica. It is an interactive screen unveiling DP World’s end-to-end solutions for the perishables supply chain.

Attendees "slide" a screen over animated visual. The sliding screen features multiple decimal points, triggering relevant content as it reaches each point. ​This mechanism provides an “x-ray” effect, illustrating DP World’s end-to-end solutions. Showcasing DP World’s expertise, extending beyond traditional port operations to cover the entire supply chain. ​

​The experience allows attendees to explore the comprehensive services DP World offers, experiencing firsthand the company’s role in ensuring the seamless journey of perishable goods from source to destination.​
