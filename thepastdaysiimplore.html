#Oil Painting #Poem Adaptation #Guó Huà #Caligraphy

This artwork was painted for an old friend who committed suicide in 2018. I searched for a lot of ancient poetries and found one that really touched me. Then I designed this painting based on my interpretation of the poetry and used oil painting to paint this Chinese traditional painting style.
