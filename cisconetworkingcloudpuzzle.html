Cisco Networking Cloud Puzzle (GPJ)
Feb 2024
Cisco Live 2024 Amsterdam

#Gamified Learning #User Engagement #Game Design #UI/UX Design #Interactive Screen #Product Promotion #Experiential Marketing

The Cisco Networking Cloud Puzzle is a riveting journey where knowledge meets fun. Users start with a series of trivia questions, unlocking ‘lives’ with each correct answer, while gaining insights into Cisco’s pioneering approach to simplifying multi-cloud systems. Then they navigate through a maze of tiles, strategically uncovering hidden images while dodging potential cloud issues. Every successful move echoes Cisco’s commitment to streamlining the multi-cloud environment, gradually revealing the masterpiece beneath.
