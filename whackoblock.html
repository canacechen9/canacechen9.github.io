Whack-a-block
Feb 05 2021
Citipointe Christian College

#Interactive Installation #Gamified Experience #Sensor System #Arduino #Capacitive Sensor

This is an interactive artwork that combines photography, digital illustration and Arduino. The artwork was inspired by the classic game, Whack-A-Mole. When an acrylic block lights up, the viewer needs to tap the block, then another block will light up. The blocks represent different pressure we have to deal with in our everyday lives. The gamification invites viewers to actually get involved in the artwork and experience the 'pressure' coming one after another.
