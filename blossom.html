Blossom
Nov 25 2019
Citipointe Christian College

#Mixed-media Art #Artistic Exploration #Organic Patterns #Printmaking #Experimental Media #Watercolour Textures

This Artwork was created after a school art camp on the Moreton Island. I used different patterns of fruits and vegetables and stamped them on a wooden board with watercolour. The patterns together look random and abstract, however, each one of them has their own unique nutrition and essence inside. This artwork tells my experience of finding the 'nutrition' of new interests and concepts of art through 'random' experiments during the camp.
