Make a Splash (GPJ)
May 2024
Click24 Amsterdam & Miami

#Immersive Projections #Interactive Space #User Experience #Brand Story #Experiential Marketing #Simulation #Twinmotion

This project invites the users to experience the magic of immersive projections at the interactive space. The users use the buttons on the wall to transform their surroundings based on audience input—one moment they’re in a swimming pool, the next, under a blue sky with clouds, or even on a thrilling roller coaster ride. As they explore, captivating projections and engaging voiceovers reveal the tools and benefits available through Click. This dynamic setup invites users to set their intentions, capture memorable moments with photos, and ignite their curiosity.

The users move around the space to discover and hear about the advantages of adopting different solutions. This activation offers a unique and memorable way to learn about the exciting opportunities Click can offer, making it an unforgettable experience for all users.
